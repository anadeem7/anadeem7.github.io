News

The new Toyota sports car is coming and has been much awaited by the people. Toyota is to reveal their new 2020 Toyota Supra at the 2019 North American International Auto Show in Detroit. This Toyota Supra will be sharing a BMW engine platform, an inline TT (Twin Turbo) 6-cylinder engine with a track focused chassis, suspension and aerodynamics. Will the 2020 Toyota Supra live up to its legacy ? Learn More

Toyota is also working on a TRD version of the new Toyota Camry and Avalon. A much sporty and good-looking sedan edition to the family. In the past TRD has been associated with only Toyota Trucks, but not anymore, the TRD version of the Camry and Avalon is expected to get chassis, suspension and appearance upgrades and will be revealed late November at the 2018 Los Angeles Auto Show.
