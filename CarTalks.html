Window problem

Hello, writing this post regarding a problem i am facing in my Toyota Camry. Once I roll down the driver's side window, and i try to roll it back up, it only goes up half way and stops. I have to wait 5-10 minutes before I can roll the window up more. Any help is welcomed for diagnosing this problem.

Reply

From: Thomas Horton
Hey Michelle,
From what you are experiencing it seems like your window regulator motor is showing age and most likely needs to be replaced because it's worn out. What's happening is, your window regulator motor is either overheating or doesn't have enough power due to usage overtime, therefore causing your window to get stuck half way. Once you let the motor cool down (waiting 5-10 minutes), it starts working again for a little bit.
